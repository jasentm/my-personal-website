JASEN MIYAMOTO

(he/him)

has always said that he never wanted to only be known as one single thing. Jasen has been a singer, an actor, a theatre director, a playwright, a podcaster, a avid fan of Dungeons & Dragons, a video game player, a video editor, a podcast editor, a manager, an assistant, an intern, an artistic collaborator, a Game Master, a witch, a recruiter, an Education Associate, a student, a teacher, and now, maybe a programmer. A proud proponent of the idea that "everything feeds everything", Jasen enjoys new horizons and the next thing that he'll learn that will, just by virtue of having learned it, make him better at a dozen other things.

Jasen is proudly queer, half-Japanese, and based in NYC.
